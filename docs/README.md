## What is SupplyLens in one paragraph?

SupplyLens consolidates inventory, supplier performance, demand forecasts, and
commodity prices into a single control tower, then layers an AI analyst on top that
explains risk and recommends ranked, executable actions. Every AI answer is computed
from your live data — it never makes numbers up. The goal is to move supply-chain
management from **reactive** (discover a stockout after it happens) to **proactive**
(surface and fix the risk before the lead-time window closes).
